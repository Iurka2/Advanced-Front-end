Is there a lock-in period?
There is no binding and you can cancel your subscription at any time. If the subscription is terminated in the middle of a month period, the excess receivable will be refunded to the tenant. (If the subscription is terminated within the first month, any excess rent will not be refunded.)
How do I cancel my subscription?
Select "Unsubscribe" in the Services fill out the form
What happens if I get my Urban-R.dk electric bike stolen?
Should it happen that your electric bike is stolen, we make sure that you get a new one quickly. We will jointly make sure that the theft is reported correctly to the police.
Should it happen that you get your Urban-R.dk electric bike, battery, or both, the following amounts are charged at deductible, provided that the bike has been locked correctly:
- Stolen bicycle without battery: DKK 2,500
- Stolen battery: DKK 4,000
- Stolen bicycle, battery and keys: DKK 15,000
What should I do if my Urban-R.dk electric bike breaks down?
Simply fill out the bike change form on the Services tab. Then we come and exchange your bike for another so you are quickly riding again. It is part of the subscription, so this service is free.
How is the Urban-R.dk electric bike equipped?
The Urban-R.dk electric bike is built in the Netherlands. Everything is made of quality materials, the electric bikes are dimensioned to ride in the Danish climate.
Engine: Bosch Active Line Plus Engine
Battery: Bosch 500Wh. Range up to 150km on a single charge.
Gear: 7 Shimano Nexus internal gears
Suspended front fork and seat post.
Point-inhibiting tires

About Urban-R

Our Misson

At Urban-R.dk, our mission is to make it easy to have an electric bike. For an amount of just DKK 749 per. month, we have made it possible to rent / lease a quality electric bicycle, without having to worry about the cost of service and maintenance.

Everything is included in the price and the electric bike is delivered directly to your address. If you have problems with your electric bike, you get a puncture, or if it needs service, you just book an appointment. We come out to your address and change the electric bike, so you are riding again without any problems.
